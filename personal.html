Self Introduction

As a part-time bus driver, my typical day unfolds in the following manner: I start my shift in the morning, greeting the busy commuters, hoping to bring a touch of brightness to their mornings. Then, I finish work around noon and return home, often dedicating my time to studying and completing assignments.

During my precious moments of leisure, I have a variety of hobbies, including watching sports, enjoying music, embracing nature, and exploring new experiences. However, my life took a significant turn two and a half years ago when my son was born. Since his arrival, my hobbies have transformed into accompanying him on his journey of growth.

I find joy in exploring the wonders of nature together, introducing him to different genres of music until we find the one he likes the most, and observing him as he plays with our Golden Retriever. I watched him confidently set forth on his path to face the world, like a little warrior, as he embarked on his first day of kindergarten, bravely venturing into the unknown without the comforting presence of his parents.

In the evenings, after my son falls asleep, my wife and I often cuddle up together, savoring moments of tranquility while watching movies. These moments provide us with a rare opportunity to relax and connect amidst our busy lives.
